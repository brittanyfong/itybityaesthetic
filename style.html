Style


Transitional Style

September 14, 2015



Fall is officially... on its way. We've had our first taste of Fall weather in Chattanooga this weekend, and it's making me seriously antsy for my favorite season to just come and stay for good. I have resisted the pumpkin season hype for as long as possible while the heat was still unyielding in its Summertime oppression, but now that the weather seems to be getting on board the Fall train, I'm going petal-to-the-metal Fall preparation mode.

In other words, Basic Bitch Mode: Engaged

Home decor and PSL's are great, but I'm having the most fun mixing cool weather elements into my style to cope with this Katy Perry weather
(You're hot then you're cold).
