Enchanted Garden of Lights

December 31, 2015



This week, I got to visit one of my favorite holiday attractions - Rock City's Enchanted Garden of Lights. This was the first time I've gone there since I used to perform holiday music with my college choir. I definitely miss singing all the holiday classics with the choir, but I did enjoy taking lots of photos with my new camera :) Enjoy, and Happy New Year's Eve!
