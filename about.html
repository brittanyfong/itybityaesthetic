Geography

          I was born in California and moved to Tennessee just in time for my awkward middle school years. I now live in Chattanooga, where I cultivate my love of music, photography, and all things cute.

A Musical Lady

          I have a degree in Vocal Music Performance. My big love is opera, but I also enjoy singing in a variety of other styles. I can also play the cello, guitar, and ukulele.

A Passion for Everything

          Over the years, I have discovered many different versions of myself. I was the little girl who sang My Heart Will Go On into the home video camera. I was the smallest 5th grader who woke up at 5am so I could get to school and pick out the biggest instrument for music class. I was the teenager who spent hours setting up amatuer photo shoots with my sister in the back yard. I was the college student who fell in love with opera and changed my major to focus on performing. I am a lady who loves languages, makeup, music, modeling, fashion, cute things, and taking photos of my food.
