Autumn Lip Colors

October 3, 2015



Now that my favorite season is really here, I think it's time for me to share some of my favorite shades of lipstick for Autumn.

If you see me out and about this season, chances are I'll be wearing one of these lip colors.


  1. Luster Matte Long-Wear Lip Color in Deep Plum Luster by Sephora
  2. Lippie Stix in Yasmin by Colourpop
  3. Ultra Matte Lip in Limbo by Colourpop
  4. Lippie Stix in Chi-Chi by Colourpop
  5. Lip Suede Velvet Lip Chubby in Mauve Over by Flower Beauty
  6. Lip Suede Velvet Lip Chubby in Red-dy to Bloom by Flower Beauty

Let's break these colors down.

I love a good deep plum/berry color. Sephora's Deep Plum Luster (left) is such a gorgeous color, perfect for pairing with neutrals or a really nice mustard yellow. Flower Beauty's Red-dy to Bloom (right) is a great option if I don't feel like being quite so vampy. It's a classic red that I like to mix with something a bit darker, like the Deep Plum Luster, to brighten it up and get that perfect berry color for Fall.

Sometimes, I like my lip color to reflect the bright colors of the leaves in Autumn. Yasmin (left) and Chi-Chi (right) are just subtly different, and I love them both equally. Yasmin is more of a red-orange coraly color, while Chi-Chi is more of a bright orange.

Neutrals are a great option for Fall when I just can't decide on a color. Colourpop's Limbo (left) is most likey going to get the most time in the spotlight this season because it is just so easy to style with my Fall wardrobe. Flower Beauty's Mauve Over (right) will be reserved for days when I want to give my lips a break and go for a more au-naturale look.


Colourpop

September 12, 2015



I recently asked a friend of mine for a reccommendation on a good coral shade of lipstick, and she suggested I check out Colourpop Cosmetics. The company is based in Los Angeles, where I'm from, and all of their Lippie Stix are $5, so I thought, "Why not?" I ordered the shade Yasmin and (im)patiently awaited its arrival.

Boy, was I pleasantly surprised! These guys pack a pigmented punch of color into their little Lippie Stix. Yasmin is absolutely everything I was hoping for. I've never really been into coral shades before, but I am definitely a fan of this one. It's the perfect mix of orangey-red that I have been dreaming of.

And can we talk about how it pops with this black and white outfit? I think it's safe to say that I will forever be a fan of Colourpop.


Liquid Lipstick

September 4, 2015



Lately, I've been obsessing over Kat Von D's liquid lipstick in the shade Lovesick.

My lips are rather pink to begin with, so it feels pretty neutral.

The lady at Sephora was not messing around when she told me this stuff was "hardcore". You can eat, drink, makeout... whatever you want to do, and it absolutely stays on until you wipe it off at the end of the day. Not only that, but it also comes off easily with a makeup wipe, unlike some other lipsticks I have. (Ahem, Revlon Color Stay Ultimate Suede...) So, basically, I am officially on board the liquid lipstick train and will definitely be trying all the shades I can get my hands on.
