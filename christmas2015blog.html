'Tis the Season

December 26, 2015



Happy Holidays! I'm sharing my Christmas experience here in an effort to entend the season of holiday cheer just a bit longer :) Merry Christmas from my little holiday world!



Our first stop is the house of my future in-laws for presents on Christmas Eve. I just love this home and all the little decorations :)



Christmas morning is spent at my mother's house - the house I lived in throughout middle and high school. My little dog lives there, too ;)



Back to my boyfriend's neck of the woods for Christmas lunch... and nap time ;)



And then it's back to my mother's for Christmas dinner.



Finally, back to our little apartment in Chattanooga, where it will still be Christmas for at least another week ;)
